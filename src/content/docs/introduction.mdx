ConvoMem is a **memory layer for conversational AI**. It captures what happens in
customer conversations, distills durable facts, and injects the relevant context back
into every future interaction — so your bots and agents never start from zero.

## Why customer memory matters

Most support stacks are stateless. Each new chat, call, or email begins without any
knowledge of the customer's history: the issue they raised last week, the plan they're
on, or the preference they stated twice already. The result is repetition, friction, and
escalations that a little context would have avoided.

ConvoMem closes that gap with three primitives:

- **Capture** — ingest conversations from chat, voice, email, and SMS.
- **Extract** — turn raw transcripts into structured, scored facts.
- **Retrieve** — surface the right context at the start of the next conversation.

## Who it's for

<Callout type="tip" title="Good fit">
ConvoMem is built for teams running support bots, voice agents, or human-in-the-loop
help desks who want continuity across sessions without building a memory pipeline
themselves.
</Callout>

You bring the conversations; ConvoMem handles extraction, durability scoring, PII
redaction, and retrieval. It's transport-agnostic and works alongside whatever LLM or
agent framework you already use.
